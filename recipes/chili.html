Chili

Description

Who doesn't love a hearty bowl of chili? This recipe is easy, which is even better!

Ingredients

  • 1 package chili seasoning mix
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 16 ounce can chili beans, with liquid
  • 2 8 ounce cans tomato sauce
  • 1 white onion, chopped fine
  • 1 green pepper, chopped fine
  • Seasonings (to taste): paprika, chili powder, cumin, cayenne

Directions

  1. Add onion, green pepper, and ground beef to pot and cook on medium-high until meat browns.
  2. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil.
  3. Cover and reduce heat to medium-low. Cook 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Serving suggestion: Serve over baked potato, topped with sour cream, shredded cheese, and diced onions or chives.
